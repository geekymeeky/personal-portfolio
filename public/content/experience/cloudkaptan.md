# Overview
As a Salesforce Developer at CloudKaptan, I am deeply involved in modernizing legacy systems, building custom financial solutions, and integrating complex cloud architectures for enterprise clients.

# The Challenge
The client’s org was burdened with heavy technical debt—relying on dozens of outdated, inefficient Process Builders and Workflow Rules. Additionally, their loan origination and tiered pricing logic was hardcoded and rigid, requiring developer intervention for every business rule change. Finally, the executive team lacked real-time visibility into their pipeline.

# The Solution & Architecture
I spearheaded a modernization and custom development initiative:
1. **Automation Migration**: I successfully migrated over 20 legacy Process Builders to highly optimized, bulkified Salesforce **Flows**, significantly reducing CPU timeout errors and improving record save times.
2. **Dynamic Pricing Engine**: I designed a metadata-driven tiered pricing engine for business loans. By leveraging Custom Metadata Types and Apex, the business team can now update pricing tiers instantly without deploying new code.
3. **Executive Dashboard**: I built a real-time revenue dashboard using **Lightning Web Components (LWC)** and integrated **Chart.js** via Static Resources for advanced data visualization.
4. **AWS Data Integrity**: I collaborated directly with the AWS data migration team to verify, validate, and scrub over 500,000 migrated records using complex SOQL aggregations and Batch Apex.

# Tech Stack & Skills
- **Salesforce Apex** (Batch, Triggers, Asynchronous)
- **Lightning Web Components (LWC)**
- **Salesforce Flows** (Record-Triggered, Screen Flows)
- **Custom Metadata Types**
- **SOQL & Data Validation**

# The ROI & Impact
> **Dramatically reduced technical debt** and system processing time by fully migrating away from deprecated Process Builders to modern Flow architectures.

> **Empowered the business team** to modify loan pricing logic instantly without IT bottlenecks via the metadata-driven engine.
